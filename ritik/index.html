financial analysts is one of the most coveted roles in the financial services industry. Financial analysts can work in both junior and senior positions within a firm and it is a position that often leads to other career opportunities in the financial services industry. A financial analyst researches macroeconomic and microeconomic conditions, gathers financial information along with company fundamentals in order to make business, sector and industry recommendations to the company
